Introduction.

  • GASOLINA is a service provider that offers logistics, transport and fuel services.
  • It was founded in 2020 by partners who are passionate about providing innovative solutions to fuel consumers whether at a small scale or large scale.
  • GASOLINA specialises in bulk supply and delivery solutions tailor- made to suit any business’s needs.

Client Portfolio

We supply and delivery in the Agricultural, Construction, Mining, Fleet, Logistics industries and any other industries with high volumes of fuel usage.

Services

Services

QUALITY PRODUCT

From our storage site to your tank. We provide premium quality diesel fuel.

INSTALLATION

We install fuel tank/s and/or fuel bowser/s equipped with pumps and required accessories on your site.

DIESEL SUPPLY

DELIVERY SOLUTIONS
